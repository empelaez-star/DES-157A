Every Picture Project V2

Whats In

My Bag?!

Lets find out...

Everything in this bag belongs to me, or at least, I like to think so. The clips, the compact, the lip gloss… I keep watch over them all. Someone’s got to make sure they don’t get lost when she’s running around again.

Nestled between loose receipts and clipped hairpins, the lip gloss waits patiently. It’s the final touch before stepping out into the world—a soft gleam that says, I’m here, and I’m ready. It carries the quiet confidence of someone who doesn’t need to shout to be seen.

My best friend gave me these fish-shaped clips just because. No reason, no special occasion—just her way of saying “this made me think of you.” They’re small and silly, but they make everything feel a little more fun. I wear them when I miss her most.

The compact was from my friend last Christmasand and it’s been in my bag ever since. It’s simple, but I love it. I didn’t realize how often I’d use it, but now it’s always there when I need it. Every time I see the reflection, I think about how lucky I am to have people who notice the little things about me.
